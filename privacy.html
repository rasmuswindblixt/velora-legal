Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Velora ("we", "our", or "the app") is built by Rasmus Windblixt. This policy explains what data is collected when you use Velora.

Data stored on your device

Your name, daily affirmation history, streak count, onboarding answers, and reminder preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Purchase data — RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier and processes purchase receipts on Apple's servers to verify your subscription status. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or any personal information from Velora. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details.

Analytics — TelemetryDeck

We use TelemetryDeck for anonymous usage analytics. TelemetryDeck is designed to be privacy-first: signals are hashed and cannot be traced back to individual users. No IP addresses, device identifiers, or personal data are transmitted. We collect events such as app launches, onboarding steps completed, and subscription interactions — solely to improve the app.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Velora schedules local notifications on your device. These are processed entirely on-device and are never sent to external servers.

No advertising or tracking

Velora contains no advertising SDKs, no third-party trackers, and does not share data with advertisers.

Your rights

You can delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Reset all data in the app. To cancel your subscription, visit your Apple ID subscription settings. For any privacy questions, contact us at rasmus.windblixt@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
